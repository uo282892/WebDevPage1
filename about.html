About us

We are an organization that was funded in 2016 by Peter Jones, a person whose love for the animals made him think about building something that could allocate a home for the worrying amount of pets that were left alone in the street.

As of today, more than 1000 animals have been rescued and allocated in safe houses in the Cork County, thanks to the Virtual Pet Adoption Service. Our intention is to expand across Ireland, so that we can carry out our mission all over our beautiful country.

List of Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to do to adopt a pet?

It is very easy, just fill the form and we will get in contact with you as soon as possible!

Where do I need to go to pick up the pet?

Just pop up by our office, the address is on the Contact page.

What do I have to do if there is any problem with my pet?

We suggest to contact a vet inmediately. In case it is very urgent, you can contact us by any of the available means and we can try to arrange something for you.
