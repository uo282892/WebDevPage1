What is the Virtual Pet Adoption Service?

The Virtual Pet Adoption Service is a website that tries to find a home for several pets. In particular, its main goal is to find the appropiate people to adopt them, as we only want the best for the pets that we are under our responsability.

In this website, you will be able to see the following:

  • The list of available pets
  • A section for the users to tell about themselves
  • Contact information

We hope that this website is useful for you to find a new friend. In case you know some people that are looking for a pet, please do not hesitate to show them our page.
